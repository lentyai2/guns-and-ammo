“High-risk” payment acceptance doesn’t get any easier than this.

Finding a partner for firearm payment processing isn’t easy. That’s because merchants who sell firearms are considered “high risk,” even by gun-friendly payment technology companies. This is due to:

The higher chargeback ratios that come with operating a firearms business.
The high dollar amount of the average firearms transaction.
Various legal and regulatory issues.
The presence of age restrictions.
The increased risk of fraud.

As a result, some processors don’t even offer firearms merchant accounts.
